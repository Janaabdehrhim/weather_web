Advice for Full Moon

  • Embrace the energy: The full moon is a time of heightened energy and emotions. Use this time to focus on your goals and intentions.
  • Practice gratitude: Take a moment to reflect on what you are grateful for in your life. This can help you attract more positive energy during the full moon.
  • Release negativity: The full moon is a powerful time for releasing negative energy and emotions. Consider writing down anything that is holding you back and then safely burning the paper to symbolize letting go.
  • Connect with nature: Spend time outside during the full moon to connect with the natural world. This can help you feel more grounded and in tune with the lunar energy.
  • Set intentions: Use the full moon as an opportunity to set intentions for the coming month. Write down your goals and aspirations, and visualize them coming to fruition.
